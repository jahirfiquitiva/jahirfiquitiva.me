About

Get to know me

My name is Jahir Fiquitiva, I was born on January 28th, 1997.
I'm a passionate and creative Android and Front-End Web developer. Born and raised in Duitama, Colombia
I code, design, translate and consult.

Ever since I was a child, I have been interested in technology, software and robotics.
After I graduated from Salesian School in 2012, I started creating, designing and programming a couple of robots.
Then I got interested in learning to develop Android apps. I started with simple things, like customization and theme apps, which you can find and download clicking here.

While I was working with robots and small Android projects, I got even more interested in programming, and since late 2014 I have been learning many things that have allowed me to start building and developing my own apps.

I am currently studying Systems and Computing Engineering (an equivalent to Computer Science) at Pedagogical and Technological University of Colombia

I consider myself a inquisitive person, always trying to learn new skills or improve existing ones. I'd also like to be part of your next project!

Additionally, if you like any of the things I do, please consider supporting my work.
