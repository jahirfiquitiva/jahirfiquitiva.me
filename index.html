Hello, I am Jahir Fiquitiva.


About

20 years old, born and grew up in Duitama, Colombia. Freelance Graphic Design enthusiast and Android developer.



I was born on January 28th, 1997.

Since I was a child I have been interesed in technology, software and robotics.
When I finished High School, I started learning about robotics, creating, designing and programming a couple of robots.
Then I got interested in learning to develop Android apps. And I decided to start with simple things, like themes and customization apps, which you can find and download here.

While I was working with robots, I got even more interested into programming, and during the last two years I have been learning lots of things that have allowed me to start building and developing my own apps. Although there's a long way to go yet. Because I want to become a Software Engineer, and that's what I plan for my future.

I am currently studying Systems and Computing Engineering in a University in Colombia, and I am putting all my efforts for everything to go well and for me to be able to make my dreams come true.

If you like any of the things I do, please consider supporting my work.
